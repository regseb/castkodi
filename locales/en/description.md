Cast&nbsp;Kodi also analyzes the pages for videos, music or external platforms integrations. For example, if a page displays a YouTube video, this video will be sent to Kodi.

To cast videos / music, three items can be added on context menu of link / page / selection of videos or audio: _Play now to Kodi_, _Play next to Kodi_ and _Queue item to Kodi_. A remote (accessible with toolbar button) is also present to cast current tab to Kodi and to: pause, go to next element, adjust the volume, â€¦

To connect the extension to Kodi, you must _Allow remote control from applications on other systems_. This option is in _Settings_ / _Services_ / _Control_ page in Kodi. You must also retrieve Kodi's _IP address_. This information is displayed in _Settings_ / _System information_ / _Summary_ page. Then enter this IP address in Cast&nbsp;Kodi _Options_.
